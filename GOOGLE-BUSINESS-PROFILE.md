# Google Business Profile Setup — Volkman Farm

A claimed Google Business Profile (GBP) is the single highest-ROI local SEO move for this site. It puts Volkman Farm into Google Maps results, the "near me" panel, and the right-rail knowledge card for branded searches. Service-area businesses (no storefront) are explicitly supported.

Estimated time: 20 minutes to submit, 1–14 days for Google to verify.

---

## Pre-flight checklist

Have these ready before you start the wizard:

- [ ] A Google account you control long-term (probably `micros@volkman.farm` if you have a Workspace, otherwise a dedicated `volkman.farm.business@gmail.com`)
- [ ] Business phone number (mobile is fine — it does not need to be a separate landline)
- [ ] Your home/farm address (only used for verification — it is NOT publicly displayed for a service-area business)
- [ ] Logo file: `/assets/logo.png` (288 KB version, square crop preferred)
- [ ] 5–10 photos: family, the grow shed, packaged clamshells, a flat of microgreens, a delivery in progress
- [ ] The exact list of 9 service neighborhoods (see below)

---

## Step-by-step

1. **Go to** [business.google.com](https://business.google.com) and sign in with your chosen Google account.
2. **Add business** → search "Volkman Farm" — confirm no duplicate exists. If none, click "Add your business to Google."
3. **Business name:** `Volkman Farm`
4. **Business category (primary):** `Farm` (this is the closest available — alternatives: `Produce wholesaler`, `Organic farm`. Pick `Farm`.)
5. **Additional categories (add up to 9):**
   - `Produce market`
   - `Health food store`
   - `Delivery service`
6. **Location:** Choose **"I deliver goods and services to my customers"** and **uncheck** "I also serve customers at my business address." This is a service-area business — your home address stays private.
7. **Service area:** Add all nine neighborhoods/cities:
   - Lake Mary, FL
   - Sanford, FL
   - Winter Springs, FL
   - Oviedo, FL
   - Casselberry, FL
   - Maitland, FL
   - Winter Park, FL
   - College Park, Orlando, FL
   - Downtown Orlando, FL
8. **Contact details:**
   - Phone: your mobile
   - Website: `https://volkman.farm`
9. **Hours:**
   - Customer-facing hours = the hours someone can reach you about an order. Suggest Mon–Fri 9am–6pm, Sat 9am–2pm, closed Sunday.
   - Add **special hours** for delivery days if you want neighbors to know exactly when their route runs.
10. **Verification:** Google will offer one of: postcard (5–14 days), phone, email, or video. **Video verification is fastest** for service-area businesses — they will ask you to walk through your grow shed showing seeds, trays, and packaged product. Have your phone ready.

## After verification — fill out the profile completely

A complete profile ranks ~2x as well as a sparse one.

- [ ] **From the business / Description (750 chars max):** Paste this draft:

We grow ten varieties of organic microgreens — arugula, beet, broccoli, cilantro, kale, kohlrabi, mustard, pea tendril, radish, and Swiss chard — and hand-deliver them weekly to neighborhoods across the Orlando metro: Lake Mary, Sanford, Winter Springs, Oviedo, Casselberry, Maitland, Winter Park, College Park, and Downtown Orlando. Every clamshell is harvested the morning of delivery, so what reaches your kitchen has never been refrigerated for days. Pricing is a flat $3.50/oz. No subscriptions, no auto-charge, cancel any week. Order at volkman.farm/microgreens.

- [ ] **Photos (upload at least 5):**
  - Logo (square)
  - Cover photo (a flat of mixed microgreens, landscape orientation)
  - Family / team photo
  - Grow shed interior
  - A packaged clamshell next to a meal
  - A delivery in progress

- [ ] **Services:** Add each variety as a service item with a $3.50/oz price.
- [ ] **Attributes:** Tick `Family-owned`, `Small business`, `Online appointments`, anything else relevant.
- [ ] **Q&A:** Pre-seed the Q&A section by posting (from your own account) and answering 3–4 common questions:
  - "Do you deliver to [neighborhood]?"
  - "How long do microgreens last in the fridge?"
  - "Is there a minimum order?"
  - "Are your microgreens organic?"

## Ongoing — weekly cadence

- **Post weekly updates** ("Posts" tab) every Monday with this week's delivery schedule and any featured variety. Posts expire after 7 days, so a missed week shows the profile as inactive.
- **Reply to every review within 48 hours** — Google's ranking algorithm weights response rate and recency.
- **Ask happy customers to leave a review.** Send the direct review link (you can find it under "Get more reviews" inside GBP) in a text after their second delivery.

---

## What this does NOT do

GBP rankings live in Google Maps and the Local Pack. They do not directly improve your blue-link search rankings — that's what the on-site SEO work covers (meta tags, JSON-LD, location pages). The two work together: GBP for map visibility, on-site SEO for organic results.
