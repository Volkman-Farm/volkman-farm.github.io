Harvested the morning we deliver. On your counter by dinner.

We're a family of five growing nutrient-dense microgreens right here in Orlando, and hand-delivering them to your neighborhood the day we cut them. No grocery middleman. No plastic clamshell that's been sitting on a shelf since Tuesday.

[**For my kitchen →**](/microgreens/) · [**For my restaurant →**](/restaurants/)

## Why our greens

**Harvested the morning of delivery.** What you eat Friday night was still growing Friday morning. Most store-bought microgreens are 7–10 days old before you open the package.

**Up to 40x the nutrients of mature greens.** A handful of broccoli microgreens has the sulforaphane of a full head of broccoli. Toss them on eggs, tacos, avocado toast, salads, sandwiches, anywhere you'd use lettuce or herbs.

**We deliver to your street, not a pickup lot.** [Lake Mary](/delivery/lake-mary/), [Sanford](/delivery/sanford/), [Winter Springs](/delivery/winter-springs/), [Oviedo](/delivery/oviedo/), [Casselberry](/delivery/casselberry/), [Maitland](/delivery/maitland/), [Winter Park](/delivery/winter-park/), [College Park](/delivery/college-park/), and [Downtown Orlando](/delivery/downtown-orlando/). One fixed day a week, porch drop, no contact needed.

## How it works

1. **Pick your green.** [Ten varieties](/greens/) at $3.50/oz: arugula, beet, broccoli, cilantro, kale, kohlrabi, mustard, pea tendril, radish, and Swiss chard.
2. **Pick your day.** We deliver to your neighborhood once a week.
3. **We text you when we're on the way.** Pay on delivery (Venmo, Zelle, or cash). [Standing weekly orders get 10% off](/subscribe/), skip any week, cancel anytime.

<details><summary>What does it cost?</summary><p>A flat $3.50 per ounce, same price for every variety. Most households start with 2 to 4 ounces per week. <a href="/subscribe/">Standing weekly orders</a> get 10% off.</p></details>

<details><summary>Do you deliver to my neighborhood?</summary><p>We deliver to nine Orlando-area neighborhoods on three rotating routes: Lake Mary, Sanford, Winter Springs, and Oviedo on Friday mornings; Casselberry, Maitland, Winter Park, and College Park on Monday and Wednesday evenings; and Downtown Orlando on Sunday afternoons. If you are not on the list yet, tell us your street when you order. We add stops to existing routes whenever it makes sense.</p></details>

<details><summary>How do I pay?</summary><p>On delivery, via Venmo, Zelle, or cash. No card on file, no auto-charge, no recurring billing software. We text you the total the day before so there are no surprises.</p></details>

<details><summary>Are your microgreens organic?</summary><p>We grow with organic, non-GMO seeds from suppliers we trust, in soilless trays, watered with filtered water. We are not USDA Organic certified, but the inputs are clean.</p></details>
