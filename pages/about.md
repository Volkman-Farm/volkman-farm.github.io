Our family, driven by a passion for healthy living and sustainability, launched a microgreens business right from our backyard. With a modest investment, we converted a simple garden shed into an efficient indoor grow space, complete with shelves, grow lights, and trays filled with nutrient-rich soil. Every family member plays a part, from managing growing cycles to packaging greens and delivering to homes and [local restaurants](/restaurants/). We specialize in high-quality, flavorful microgreens like [cilantro](/greens/cilantro/), [pea shoots](/greens/pea/), [arugula](/greens/arugula/), and [beet greens](/greens/beet/) (see [all ten varieties](/greens/)) grown using organic seeds and clean, filtered water. Our mission is to nourish our community across the [Orlando metro](/delivery/) with fresh, locally grown food you can feel good about. Ready to taste the difference?
