Our family, driven by a passion for healthy living and sustainability, launched a microgreens business right from our backyard. With a modest investment, we converted a simple garden shed into an efficient indoor grow space, complete with shelves, grow lights, and trays filled with nutrient-rich soil. Every family member plays a part, from managing growing cycles to packaging greens and delivering to homes and local restaurants. We specialize in high-quality, flavorful microgreens like cilantro, pea shoots, arugula, and beet greens grown using organic seeds and clean, filtered water. Our mission is to nourish our community with fresh, locally grown food you can feel good about. Ready to taste the difference? Place your order today!
