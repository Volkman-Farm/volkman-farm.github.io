Five Volkmans live in Sanford. Two parents, three kids, German on one side, Florida on the other, and a shed out back full of trays. We started growing microgreens because we wanted better food on our own table. The route grew from there.

The shed is a converted garden shed with shelves, grow lights, and trays. Albert built it. The kids help pack. Everyone has a job during a harvest morning. We grow [ten varieties](/greens/) on rotation, using organic non-GMO seeds and filtered water for irrigation. The seeds we trust, the water we trust, and the families on the route are the same families we run into at the grocery store.

We deliver to nine neighborhoods across the [Orlando metro](/delivery/) on three rotating routes. Lake Mary, Sanford, Winter Springs, and Oviedo on Friday mornings. Casselberry, Maitland, Winter Park, and College Park on Monday and Wednesday evenings. Downtown Orlando on Sunday afternoons. If your street isn't on the list yet, tell us when you order. We add stops to existing routes whenever it makes sense.

We also work with [local restaurants](/restaurants/) on standing wholesale orders. Same family, same shed, larger trays.
