## How it works

Tell us once what you'd like each week (variety, amount, neighborhood) and we put it on the route every week without you having to reorder.

1. **Place your first order** through our regular order form. Check the "make this a weekly standing order" box.
2. **We text you the day before each delivery** to confirm. Want to skip this week? Reply "skip." Want extra? Reply with what you need.
3. **Pay weekly on delivery.** Venmo, Zelle, or cash. No card on file, no auto-charge, no recurring billing software.

That's it. The whole thing runs on text messages and trust.

## What you save

Standing-order customers get **10% off the per-ounce rate**, $3.15/oz instead of $3.50/oz, on every delivery, week after week.

A 4oz weekly order saves $1.40 per week, about $72/year. The bigger savings are in time: no more "did I order microgreens this week?" Tuesday-morning panic.

## Why we built this

Most of our customers were already buying the same thing every week. The standing order just removes the friction: fewer texts back and forth, fewer "oh shoot, I forgot to order" weeks, easier delivery planning for us, lower price for you.

It also lets us plan our seeding schedule. When we know exactly what's going out the door every Friday and Wednesday, we waste less seed, water, and tray space, and we pass that efficiency back to you as the discount.

## What if my schedule changes?

Reply "skip" to any reminder text and that week's delivery is off, no charge, no penalty. Going on vacation for the month? Reply "pause" and we'll stop reminders until you say "resume."

If you decide standing orders aren't for you, just tell us. There's no contract to cancel, no website portal to navigate.
